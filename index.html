I look forward to continue developing my skills across these technologies as they become more and more relevant to future developments helping to shape our world, decisions, and environment.

Please see below for links to some of my latest class projects and also, in 2016 I completed an award-winning Story Map using Esri's online program concerning Antarctica.

Colleagues and classmates know me as hard working, a quick learner and problem solver. I have experience in HTML, CSS, Python, Javascript (including Leaflet and D3) and ArcGIS, and am constantly learning more every day.
