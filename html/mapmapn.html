MapMapn Studio & Lab

MapMapn Studio and Lab is a web cartography sandbox. I was inspired to create this site when over the summer of 2018 I found myself struggling with the graphic design aspects and the sometimes overwhelming resources available online.

MapMapn is by no means exhaustive and there are a multitude of avenues that I won't be exploring in this format. I also have simplified some things for use in the 'lab' and much more impressive results can be created with specific javascript (and real data sets).

I also hope to build out the 'Studio' side with options for static maps, both digital and print viewing.

HTML, CSS, Javascript, Jquery, Bootstrap, Webpack, Leaflet, axios, Node.js
