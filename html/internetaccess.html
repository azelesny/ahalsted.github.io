Internet Access in Sub-Saharan Africa

Created a proportional symbols map via a spatiotemporal visualization representing a regional geographic phenomemonon. Parameters for creation included interactivity by allow users to customize their experience with the map, empower users with control over the map, and create discussion around the issue.

Internet access is part of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. Sub-Saharan nations have extremely low numbers for internet access and face considerable infrastructure difficulties.

HTML, CSS, Javascript, Jquery, GeoJSON, Leaflet, Mapbox Tile
