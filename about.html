About

"Go get the atlas!" was a common phrase in my house growing up - and while "Look it up on Google Maps" may have replaced it - the reason we said it is still the same. Spatial understanding of a place can make add so much texture and detail to a news article, a book, or a place just mentioned in passing. It was only in the past few years that I gained exposure to this wonderful world most people think is a dead career (How many times can you hear "but haven't we mapped everything already?") and I am hooked.

I am currently a Master's student in University of Wisconsin-Madison's online professional masters for Web Mapping and GIS Development. I am enjoying learning not only about cartography and geospatial technologies but also have a growing interest in programming and coding.I look forward to continuing to develop my skills across these technologies as they become more and more relevant to future developments helping to shape our world, decisions, and environment.
